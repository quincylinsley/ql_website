Cooking Adventures

Some of the best things I've eaten are the things I've made myself. Being a bit lazy and not one for extremely technical dishes, you can be sure that these dishes can be pulled off by just about anyone. It's also important to note that many dishes cannot be done at home to the same standard as a restaurant. I know it. These are dishes that are not only still good at home, but potentially better for the ability to curtail them to your exact liking as well as being able to eat the dish when it's at peak freshness.
