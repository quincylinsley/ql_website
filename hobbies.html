Lazy Hobbies

It's important to distinguish between lazy hobbies and non-lazy hobbies so that when determining which hobby to pursue you can select appropriately. Pursuing an active hobby, either physically or mentally, may have negative results if the endeavor takes place during a time of fatigue. Below I have created a list for myself of hobbies that require very little from me either physically or mentally.

  • tv show or movie with simple plot
  • swinging
  • puzzle
  • neighborhood walk
  • carwash and drink run
  • people watching in the park
  • coffee shop hang
  • movie theatre
  • meditation
